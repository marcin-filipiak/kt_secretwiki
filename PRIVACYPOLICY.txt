Thank you for choosing SecretWiki! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect:

    Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and encrypted password to ensure secure access.

    Notes and Content: Your notes and content created within SecretWiki are stored securely on your device. We do not access or store this information.

    Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data to enhance the application's performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information:

    Access and Authentication: Your account information is used to authenticate and provide access to SecretWiki.

    Improving Our Service: Usage data helps us understand how you interact with the application, allowing us to make improvements.

3. Data Security:

    Encryption: Your data is encrypted to ensure confidentiality. Only you, with your password, can access your information.

    Secure Storage: We employ industry-standard measures to store data securely on your device.

4. Data Sharing:

    No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties.

    Linked Notes: While SecretWiki allows you to link notes together, this data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

5. Updates and Communication:

    App Updates: Periodically, we may release updates to improve functionality and security.

    Communication: We may send important announcements or updates regarding the application, but we won't spam you.

6. Your Choices:

    Data Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data within the application.

    Opt-Out: You can opt-out of non-essential communications.

7. Children's Privacy:

    Not Intended for Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Policy:

    Notification: If we make changes to this policy, we will notify you through the application or via email.

By using SecretWiki, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@noweenergie.org

Thank you for trusting SecretWiki to safeguard your information!
